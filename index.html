About the data & caveats

About the data

Letters, petitions, and collective statements signed by UC faculty, standardized from public sources (sign-on sites, senate records, news archives, PDFs). Identity across letters is matched by normalized name: homonyms may merge and spelling variants may split — this most affects the signatory network. Signatures are long-format (a person appears once per letter signed); cross-letter overlap is the subject of this site. Letter dates follow the document or its sign-on log; dates marked approx are inferred from coverage. Background notes and outcomes were researched against contemporary reporting (last verified June 2026) — follow each letter's source links for the records themselves. Names are drawn from public letters; rosters reflect the captured snapshot, and sign-on letters may have continued to grow.
